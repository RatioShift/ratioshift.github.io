I Joined a Web Development Bootcamp: My Experience with Programming Hero

Published on Nov 2, 2024 • schedule

They say the best way to predict the future is to create it. That’s why I finally took the leap and joined the Web Development Bootcamp with Programming Hero.

If you're in the tech community, you've probably heard of Jhankar Mahbub and his "crazy" level of support system. After following their content for a while, I decided to become a formal student. The course is intense, structured, and designed to take you from absolute zero to a professional developer in 6 months.

The Structure: Milestones & Modules

What I love most about this bootcamp is how it’s organized. It’s not just a random list of videos. It’s divided into Milestones, and each milestone contains several Modules. This "reward-based" system keeps you motivated—you can't just skip ahead; you have to earn your progress.

Current Status: I am currently in the middle of the journey! This means some of my notes are still "Coming Soon" as I unlock new milestones.

Why I am building this "Notebook"

As a student, the amount of information is massive. To stay organized, I started creating detailed notes in Google Docs for every module I complete. I realized that these notes could help others too. Whether you are a fellow student or someone curious about the curriculum, you are welcome to use my resources.
